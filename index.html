SIP Calculator

What is a SIP Calculator?

A SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) Calculator is a financial tool that helps you estimate the potential returns on your regular mutual fund investments...

How to Use this Tool?

  1. Enter the amount you want to invest every month.
  2. Select the investment duration (in years).
  3. Provide the expected annual return rate.
  4. (Optional) Enable the “Adjust for Inflation” option and enter an inflation rate.
  5. Click on Calculate to view the maturity value, CAGR, and inflation-adjusted results.

Benefits of Using SIP Calculator

  • Helps in financial planning by showing how small investments grow over time.
  • Allows you to set realistic investment goals.
  • Makes it easier to compare different scenarios by adjusting inputs.
  • Provides inflation-adjusted values to understand the real worth of your money.
